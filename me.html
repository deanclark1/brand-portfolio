About

Lady-Omega is the CEO of Ampersand Technologies Ltd, a software development company that connects people and technology by conceptualising, building and delivering quality, engaging and disruptive software products and information technology solutions that solve real world problems. Over the past 4 years, she leads a team of 10 developers (all under 30 years) with over 10 years experience in building software working with 34 companies across 16 different industries in four geographic regions and continents (with clients spanning Ghana, Nigeria, the United Kingdom and the United States) dedicated to solving problems one software application at a time....

After completion of her 1st degree in Geomatic Engineering at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, she followed her passion for technology at Meltwater Entrepreneurial School of Technology, where she learned how to code, develop her business and communication skills in building a world class software company. On top of building a software company of her own, Lady-Omega is cofounder of STEMbees, an NGO that has introduced over 1000 girls to Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM), empowering and mentoring them to explore their passions from a young age. She has supported girls from Wesley Girls, Archbishop Potter Girls and Methodist Girls High School to represent Ghana at International Robotics Competition like First Global. She also serves as the Youth Chairman at the Young Women Christian Association (Ghana), an NGO that supports young women with the Word of God, skills and education needed for them to succeed.

Her motivation is to first believe in yourself, you can do it - And smile, it makes things easier.
